"Reading Sprint Maps"


Because you have been here before the process is a bit simpler:
  1. We don't need your 'consent' or your demographic information.
  2. We don't train or test your map reading ability.
  3. Each set of trial has an introduction followed by a number of real trials. Note that you see the map for a shorter time, (because you are experienced). If you find you aren't sure of the correct answer, just guess (It may surprise you how often you are correct!).
  4. At the end, we'll show you a summary of your results. If you can, send me a quick email when you are done and I can update you on what we have learnt so far.

As before, the total time taken should be about 20 minutes. Please don't use the "back" button on your browser or close the window until you reach the end. Doing this is very likely to break the experiment which means that I will loose my data and you may get asked to do it again. If something does go wrong, please contact me, and I will do what I can to fix it! When you're ready to begin, click on the "start" button below.
